01 / The problem

Bold ideas die waiting for a first dollar.

To win a major grant, a scientist has to show early results. But producing those results costs money the scientist doesn't have yet. New researchers get stuck in that loop, and promising ideas never get tested.

02 / The move

We fund the first spark.

CRF operates like a venture firm for science. We place seed capital with rising stars at the point of maximum risk and maximum leverage, so a hypothesis can become primary data, and primary data can become a career of discovery.

03 / The result

180+ scientists across the gap.

Researchers whose earliest work we backed have gone on to reshape how cancer is understood, detected, and treated. Their discoveries compound. So does your gift.
